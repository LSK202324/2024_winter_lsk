It is our great pleasure to announce that the 2024 LSK winter conference will be held at Sungkyunkwan University, Seoul, on 23 November 2024. The theme is “Experimental Approaches to the Study of Language”.

The details are as follows:

  • Plenary Speaker
  • Dr. Heejeong KO (Seoul National University)
  • Dr. Tae-Jin YOON (Sungshin Women's University)

  • Important Dates
  • Abstract submission (Extended): 25 Oct 2024
  • ​Notification of acceptance: 30 Oct 2024
  • The final version of the conference program announced: 13 Nov 2024
  • ​LSK Winter Conference: 23 Nov 2024

  • Registration Fee
  • Instructors: 30,000 won
  • Students: 10,000 won

    • If there’s any inquiry or comment, please send a note to the Program Committee (lsk202324@gmail.com). We’re looking forward to seeing you at the conference, and thank you very much for your cooperation.

Dear Colleagues,

It is our great pleasure to announce that the 2023 LSK winter conference will be held at Kyung Hee University, Seoul, on 9 December 2023, preceded by Young Scholar Symposium (at Korea University, 8 December 2023). The theme is “Language uses, analyses, and research methodologies in the era of A.I.” and it also features a session of Asian language studies with invited speakers from Japan, Philippines, and Taiwan.

The details are as follows:

  • Plenary Speaker
  • Dr. Ira Noveck (Centre national de la recherche scientifique (CNRS))
  • Isolating the pragmatic contribution of utterances and even words

  • Invited speakers
  • Dr. Hiromu Sakai (Waseda University)
  • Neural representation of semantic concepts revealed by MEG
  • Dr. Shirley Dita (De La Salle University)
  • Tagalog response system: issues and challenges
  • Dr. Shu-ing Shyu (National Sun Yat-sen University, Kaohsiung)
  • Mandarin Ye: Additivity or Scalarity?

  • Important Dates
  • 11 Sept - 27 Oct 2023​      Abstract submission (Extended)
  • 6 Nov 2023​                         ​Notification of acceptance
  • 13 Nov 2023​      ​      ​      ​   ​ The final version of the conference program announced
  • 8 Dec 2023                         ​​Young Scholar Symposium
  • 9 Dec 2023                         ​LSK Winter Conference

    • If there’s any inquiry or comment, please send a note to the Program Committee (lsk202324@gmail.com) or Iksoo Kwon (the conference organizer, kwoniks@hufs.ac.kr). We’re looking forward to seeing you at the conference, and thank you very much for your cooperation.

    Sincerely yours,

    Iksoo Kwon
    On behalf of the organizing committee
